The Bamboo Cross Bridge in Majuli river island is an engineering marvel and a cultural symbol of the region. Constructed entirely of bamboo, this bridge stretches over a wide water body and offers visitors breathtaking views of the island's natural beauty.

The bridge is a testament to the skill and craftsmanship of the local community, who have used bamboo as a building material for centuries. Its design reflects the principles of sustainability and harmony with nature, making it a unique example of eco-friendly construction.

The Bamboo Cross Bridge is not just a functional structure but also a cultural icon that celebrates the rich heritage and traditions of the region. It is a must-visit destination for those seeking to experience the natural beauty and cultural significance of Majuli.
