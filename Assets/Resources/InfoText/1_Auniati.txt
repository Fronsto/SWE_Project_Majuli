The Auniati Satra is one of the most famous Satra in Majuli. It was established in 1653 by Niranjan Pathakdeva. The Auniati Satra is famous for Paalnaam and Apsara Dance. The Paalnaam is one of the most significant religious festivals of the Satra. It is celebrated during the month of November.
In the main prayer hall of the Satra, prayers continue from morning to evening and this tradition has been uninterruptedly happening for the last 350 years. The Sattriya dance performance held here is dedicated to Lord Govinda in the main prayer hall (Naam Ghar).
